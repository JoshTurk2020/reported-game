Reported Speech Game - Summer Holiday Plans

Player 1's turn

John and Emily were discussing their summer holiday plans. John said, "I think we should go to the beach this year."

John suggested that they should go to the beach that year.

Emily replied, "But we went to the beach last year. Let's try something different."

Emily pointed out that they had gone to the beach the previous year and proposed trying something different.

Emily said, "I can't believe we finally agreed on a destination!"

Emily expressed her surprise that they had finally agreed on a destination.

John replied, "It's going to be the best summer holiday ever!"

John declared that it was going to be the best summer holiday ever.
